    Uranus - PNL

    Two brothers :

    PNL’s ‘Uranus’ music is part of the 2016 Dans la légende album. PNL, being a music group composed of two French rappers , Ademo and N.O.S , two brothers. They composed, wrote , produced , several of their sounds and we created several labels PNL records, QLF records in 2014 and 2015.

    Some figures and data :

    In 2016 they released their album Dans la légende, which is considered to be “cloud rap”, a monumental success. Several sounds like Naha , Béné, In legend, Onizuka become very quickly cult and inked sounds in the history of French rap. The album sold more than 1 million copies. The album received positive reviews, also received the gold record with 51,487 copies sold in 1 week and much later in December received the triple platinum record the equivalent of 300,000 sales.

    Musical content :

    PNL uses very melodious musical sounds that can make the listener think and travel by mixing with «soft» and «spatial» sounds that make the mind travel and make the void. In their texts they do a lot of references, several mangas including GTO, Dragon Ball and Naruto. This allows us to situate ourselves in a context and be thoughtful, to travel their music between the animated world of mangas and the real world.
