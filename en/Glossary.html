Glossary

Acclimatize :

Acclimatization refers to the physiological adaptation of a living organism transposed to a geographical region different from its area of ​​origin.

Canton :

A township is a type of lower-level territory than a state.

Cloud rap :

Also called trillwave, is a subgenre of rap, characterized in particular by a musical structure that is slower and more airy than traditional rap.

Record :

A certification record is an award given to an artist to highlight that an album or single has sold a certain number of copies.

Scholar :

Synonym of scholar.

Land Iguana :

A unique reptilian because it is found only in the Galapagos Islands.

Unproductive :

Who does not produce, does not bring anything.

Label :

Record production company.

Premature death :

The premature mortality rate is an indicator that reflects the state of health of a population.

Science fiction :

Literary and artistic genre that describes a future state of the world by extrapolating data from science or technology.
